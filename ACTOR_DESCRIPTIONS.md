# Actor Descriptions

Based on the Use Case Diagrams, here are the definitions for the system actors.

| # | Actor Name | Definition |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | **User** (Normal User) | A standard user who creates and manages their personal profile, shares multimedia posts (photos, videos) with captions, interacts with others through likes, comments, and chats (1-on-1 or groups). They can also manage collections, follow friends, join community groups, and report inappropriate content. |
| 2 | **Creator** (Professional User) | A user who has upgraded to a professional account. Inheriting all capabilities of a Normal User, they additionally have access to a professional dashboard for insights, can switch to Pro mode, and manage advertisement campaigns to promote their content. |
| 3 | **Admin** (Administrator) | A system administrator responsible for maintaining the platform's integrity. They moderate users and content (accepting/denying reports), monitor system health and performance metrics, and view application logs to ensure smooth operations. |
